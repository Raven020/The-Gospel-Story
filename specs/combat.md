# Combat System Specification

## Standard Combat
- Traditional JRPG turn-based battle system
- Active party size: 5 (Jesus + 4 disciples)
- Spiritually themed abilities replacing conventional RPG actions:
  - **Prayer** — healing, buffs, and support actions
  - **Miracles** — powerful offensive/utility abilities (casting out demons, calming storms, etc.)
  - **Truth** — speaking truth to enemies; effective against deception-based foes
  - **Scripture** — core combat mechanic; choose the correct Scripture verse to counter enemies
  - **Items** — consumables (bread, oil, etc.) with thematic flavor
  - **Defend/Wait** — standard defensive options
- Encounters represent spiritual conflict, not literal violence
- Each disciple has unique abilities tied to their character:
  - Peter: Strength/Nets — physical damage/tank
  - Andrew: Recruit/Rally — support buffer
  - James: Thunder/Zeal — offensive powerhouse
  - John: Love/Devotion — healer
  - Philip: Inquiry — enemy analysis
  - Bartholomew: Sincerity/Purity — cleanser/debuff removal
  - Matthew: Gold/Resources
  - Thomas: Doubt/Detection

## Enemy Types

### Demons / Evil Spirits
- Literal spiritual enemies encountered in the overworld
- Defeated through Scripture selection, Prayer, Miracles, and Truth
- Boss-level demons for major story encounters (e.g., Legion, Satan's temptations)

### Abstract Spiritual Forces
- Personified forces: Doubt, Fear, Temptation, Pride, Greed, Deception, etc.
- Random encounter enemies defeated through the same combat mechanics
- Thematically tied to the current arc's narrative themes

### Healing Encounters
- People appear needing healing (the sick, the afflicted, the possessed)
- Resolved through Miracles and Prayer rather than offensive combat
- Available when Jesus or disciples have been given authority to heal

## Random Encounters
- Pokemon tall-grass style — encounters trigger while walking the overworld and routes
- Frequency to be tuned during playtesting (not overwhelming)
- Mix of demon/spiritual force combat and healing encounters

## Scripture-Selection Combat
- Core mechanic across all encounter types, not just boss fights
- Player presented with multiple Scripture verses and must choose the correct one to counter the opponent's challenge
- Wrong selections do not cause failure (God's providence) but affect stats and morale
- Correct selections grant bonus stat rewards and morale boosts

## Debate / Riddle Battles
- Used for Pharisee confrontations and Temple teacher encounters
- Logic and Scripture knowledge as the combat mechanic
- Dialogue-tree style with timed or weighted responses

## Player Agency
- Choices affect stats and morale, never the story outcome
- God's providence ensures the narrative proceeds regardless of player performance
- Meaningful consequences: wrong choices create noticeable combat difficulty shifts and visibly affect morale, but players can always recover
- Better choices lead to stronger party stats and higher morale
